About JFO

Founded in 2012 at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, the Jasmine Field Orchestra (JFO) is a vibrant, student-led music community. We are built on a foundation of inclusivity and a shared passion for music, connecting diverse cultures and people. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive platform for musical exploration, cultural exchange, and personal growth.

Our Music Divisions

Symphony Orchestra

Exploring the richness of the symphonic world, from classical masterpieces to contemporary works.

Chinese Folk Music

Showcasing the enchanting melodies and unique charm of traditional Chinese instruments.

Choir

Celebrating the power of collective voices through intricate and moving choral arrangements.

Live Bands Division

Feeling the pulse of modern music with high-energy pop, rock, and contemporary performances.

Become a Part of Our Symphony

JFO welcomes all students with a passion for music, regardless of experience. If you're ready to share your musical journey, we invite you to join our family. Our application process is open throughout the year.
