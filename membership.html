Frequently Asked Questions

What do membership dues cover?
Dues help fund rehearsal venue bookings, sheet music licensing, concert production, equipment maintenance, and community events. As a student-run organization, membership dues are what keep JFO running each semester.
Is there a deadline to pay dues?
Dues are typically due within the first few weeks of each semester. Check our News & Announcements page or your division's group chat for the exact deadline each term.
Can I attend rehearsals before paying?
Yes! New members are welcome to try out a few rehearsals before committing. We want you to feel confident about joining before you pay.
What if I cannot afford dues?
We never want finances to be a barrier to making music. If you're experiencing financial hardship, please reach out to us at hello@jfouiuc.com to discuss options.
Are membership dues refundable?
Membership dues are treated as charitable contributions to support JFO's mission and are non-refundable.
